About Us

At Sow & Serve, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the agricultural sector through technology-driven food optimization. Our platform is designed to bridge the gap between farmers, distributors, NGOs, and consumers, ensuring efficient food production, reduced waste, and fair market practices. With features like AI-driven demand forecasting, a digital marketplace, waste management solutions, weather predictions, and an AI chatbot, we empower farmers with real-time insights, better trade opportunities, and sustainable farming techniques. Our mission is to enhance food security, minimize resource wastage, and create a smarter, more connected agricultural ecosystem. Join us in building a future where technology meets agriculture for a more sustainable world!
